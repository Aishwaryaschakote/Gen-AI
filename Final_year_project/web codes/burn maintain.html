Today’s Workout Plan
Warm-up & Mobility
5 minutes of light cardio and full-body mobility exercises
Marching or Brisk Walk
5–7 minutes of steady-paced walking or marching in place
Bodyweight Squats
2–3 sets of 12 squats to maintain lower-body strength
Wall or Knee Push-Ups
2 sets of 10–12 reps to maintain upper-body strength
Standing Side Leg Raises
2 sets of 12 reps per leg for balance and hip strength
Core Hold (Plank or Seated Hold)
Hold for 20–30 seconds, repeat 2 times
Cool Down & Stretching
4–5 minutes of full-body stretching and breathing exercises
